About the Artist

Amber Moon is a professional dancer, ceramic artist, and visual artist. She recently moved to Missoula, MT from Helena, MT to finish a Multidisciplinary Studies degree focusing on Media Arts, Entertainment Management, and Theater Tech at the University of Montana. She is very interested in pursuing Graphic Design. This website is a culmination of her work in pursuance of a certificate in Digital Design. She humbly asks that you forgive the web design, as she is brand new to coding.
